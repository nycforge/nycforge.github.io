Minecraft and the Forge mod loader

Our mission is to make parents more knowledgable and confident about configuring and modifying their Minecraft setup at home. The ideal setup for most players will have an installation of Forge - the popular mod loader for Minecraft that enables their kids to run mods that they download from the internet.

If at any point, you feel overwhelmed or confused by any of the lingo, look to the great resources for parents section of this website
